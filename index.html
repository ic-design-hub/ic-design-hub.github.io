By consolidating this information, we aspire to facilitate greater connectivity and collaboration within the IC Design academic community.

Should any faculty member have transitioned to a different institution than those indicated herein, or if any distinguished scholar wishes to be included in this esteemed directory, we earnestly invite you to reach out to the authors of this list via email. Your cooperation in keeping this resource current and comprehensive is greatly appreciated.
